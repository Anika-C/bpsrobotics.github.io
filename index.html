Welcome

FIRST (For Inspiration and Recognition of Science and Technology). We support STEM and STEAM. Our organization is for helping set up and maintain FIRST Robotics teams. With our experience we help guide new team setup and organization of their finances as well as the technical needs. Our teams run workshops during the summer for both kids, mentors, and coaches.
