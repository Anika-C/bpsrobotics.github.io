Welcome to 2898 Flying Hedgehogs Robotics

Team 2898, the Flying Hedgehogs, is based out of the Beaverton School District School of Science and Technology. We have existed since 2009, and have played in three years since that time, 2009, 2012, and 2013. We will be rejoining the world of FRC robotics for the 2017 season.


Our sister team, 1510 The Wildcats, had such a large percentage of students from the School of Science and Technology that we split and became our own team. Team 1510 has helped our team become a reality. We share resources including a machine shop, storage areas, and robot designs.
