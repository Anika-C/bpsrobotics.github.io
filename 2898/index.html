Welcome 2898 Robotics

Team 2898, the Flying Hedgehogs, is based out of the Beaverton School District. We have been around for 2 years before our break. We came out of team 1510 Wildcats. We had so much participation that we split into two teams. We had such a high percentage of our students from the School of Science and Technology (SST) that we were made into an independent team. Team 1510 has greatly helped us get started. We share the same shop, come from the same schools, and help each other make robots. Being on team 2898 is a great experience. The 2898 Flying Hedgehogs robotics team is a great team with great potential. We hope to show our potential next year. See you all at the competition.
